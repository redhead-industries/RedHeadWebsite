Our mission

RedHead exists to support the development, promotion, and sharing of open-source software, privacy-respecting digital tools, community-driven technology, and open research for public benefit.

Privacy is a right

Our products are designed to protect people from tracking, exploitation, and manipulation.

Transparency builds trust

Code, decisions, and goals stay open so the community can see and shape the work.

Freedom of speech

Open access to knowledge and expression is essential for progress and equality.

Community over profit

We operate without the purpose of gain. Resources are reinvested to advance the mission.

What we do

RedHead organizes its work into branches and initiatives that focus on building ambitious, open, and privacy-first technology.

RedHead Technologies

RedHead Open-Research

Public, collaborative research exploring new tools and ideas that benefit society.

Our story

From a small publishing effort to a privacy-first open-source movement, RedHead keeps evolving with the community.

2015

Founded by Matthew DaLuz as a book and comic publishing company.

2019-2020

Built the fan-made open-source project Vib-Ribbon+, shaping our modern open-source philosophy.

2024

RedHead relaunched with a renewed focus on transparent, community-powered technology.

2026

Incorporation documents prepared under Ontario's Not-for-Profit Corporations Act, with registration targeted by February 2026.

Governance

RedHead Industries is structured to operate without share capital and without the purpose of gain, ensuring resources serve the mission.

Non-profit purpose

  • All resources are used to advance open-source and open research work.
  • No income is paid out for personal benefit beyond what ONCA allows.
  • On dissolution, assets go to similar non-profit organizations.

Leadership

  • Founder: Matthew DaLuz.
  • Current director and officer appointments: Matthew DaLuz.
  • Membership structure defined by the board of directors.
